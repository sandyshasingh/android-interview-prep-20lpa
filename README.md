Android Interview Preparation Guide (4+ Years Experience)

This repository contains a 2-week structured roadmap designed to help mid-level Android developers (4+ years experience) prepare for high-paying Android roles (20 LPA+).

The guide focuses on the most frequently asked Android interview topics, system design concepts, and coding preparation required to crack top product companies.

** Topics Covered

The preparation plan includes:

Android Architecture (MVVM, Clean Architecture)

Kotlin Deep Concepts

Coroutines & Flow

Jetpack Components

Dependency Injection

Android System Design

Performance Optimization

Memory Leaks & Debugging

Multithreading

DSA patterns frequently asked in Android interviews

Real Android interview questions

** 2-Week Preparation Strategy

The roadmap is structured into 14 days, where each day focuses on specific areas like:

Day 1–3
Core Android fundamentals and Kotlin deep dive.

Day 4–6
Jetpack libraries, architecture patterns, and best practices.

Day 7–9
Concurrency, coroutines, flows, and performance optimization.

Day 10–12
System design, scalability, and real interview questions.

Day 13–14
Mock interviews and revision.

🎯 Who Is This For

This guide is designed for:

Android Developers with 3–6 years of experience

Engineers preparing for product company interviews

Developers targeting 15–30 LPA roles
